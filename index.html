about us

We are a commercial fitness service provider designed and dedicated to offering affordable, effective, efficient, and safe fitness training services and facilities to low & middle-class communities. Understanding the need and the necessity of the Fitness industry for healthier communities and the growing demand and opportunities for fitness activities as a lifestyle due to the impact of socioeconomic conditions on the health of communities, CORE GYM SA was initiated and created by Mr. ROBERT & Mr. MOUFRA in January 2022 and the two partners are the sole founders and owners of CORE GYM SA.
